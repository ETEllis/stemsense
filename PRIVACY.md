# StemSense Privacy

StemSense does not collect, retain, sell, or transmit personal data.

The Safari extension runs only on YouTube domains granted by the user. It reads the active page's HTML video playback position solely to apply a local ten-second seek. It has no analytics, account system, advertising identifier, background server, or browsing-history store.

The focused player sends video playback requests directly to YouTube through Google's supported embedded-player interface. YouTube's own service and privacy terms apply to that playback.
